Projects

Department of Defence RWD & UI website redesign

Redesign of this Australian Government website, that needed to serve both the Australian public, as well as members of the ADF/APS. focusing on AAA accessibility, responsive web design (RWD), clear information architecture (IA) and purpose through intentional UI style.

lessCOVIDstress: mobile app research and design

Aimed at post-pandemic travellers, lessCOVIDstress is a mobile app that provides digestible, up-to-date information about covid at their destination/s. Designed to reduce time and stress of searching across the internet for accurate covidsafe info across many destinations & languages.

ArtsLaw nonprofit website redesign

ArtsLaw is an independent, non-profit that exists to provide legal resources and advice for artists in Australia. Their website was quite content heavy, so we sought to create a design that was more accessible, responsive, and felt more like a website for artists through thoughtful UI style.
